# Troubleshooting Scenario 1: Morning Routine

This guide covers common issues you might encounter while playing **Scenario 1: Morning Routine** and provides steps to resolve them.

---

## 1. Right Hand is Invisible or Missing
**Issue:** When the scenario starts, you cannot see your right hand or use it to interact with objects.

**Solution:**
* **This is intentional.** In this scenario, the protagonist is missing their right arm. Your right hand will remain invisible until you complete the objective to equip the prosthetic arm.
* However, a guide ray should still be visible when you point at UI menus. 
* To resolve this, simply progress through the scenario: proceed to the shower room and **equip the prosthetic arm** using your left hand. Once equipped, your right hand will become fully visible and functional.

---

## 2. Difficulty Adjusting the Shower Temperature
**Issue:** You are unable to turn on the shower or adjust the temperature dial to fill the completion gauge.

**Solution:**
* **Grabbing the Dial:** Ensure you are using your **left hand** and that you are close enough to the showerhead. Press and hold the interact button (Trigger/Grip) to grab the dial.
* **Turning the Dial:** While holding the dial, physically rotate your controller. Turn to the **left for hot water** and to the **right for cold water**.
* **Gauge Not Filling:** If the gauge stops filling, it means the water temperature is no longer optimal. Keep adjusting the dial slightly left or right until the gauge starts filling up again. 
* **Turning it Off:** Remember to press the button on the showerhead again to stop the water once the gauge is full.

---

## 3. Prosthetic Arm Won't Attach
**Issue:** You have reached the prosthetic arm but it won't equip when you try to grab it.

**Solution:**
* You must use your **left hand** to grab the prosthetic arm. 
* Make sure you are standing close enough to the arm hanging in the shower room. Aim at it until the interaction highlight appears, then press the interact button.
* **Save System Synchronization:** If you have completed all previous conditions and the arm still won't attach, there might be a temporary issue with the AWS save system. Please exit to the main menu and log back in. Upon returning, you should be able to equip the arm normally.

---

## 4. Issues Interacting with Mary (AI Character)
**Issue:** Mary is not responding to your voice, or the virtual keyboard is not appearing.

**Solution:**
* **Microphone Issues:** Check if your microphone is muted or if "Speech to Text" is disabled in the Quick Menu settings.
* **OpenAI Connection:** If the server connection drops, the game will automatically switch to offline mode. In this case, use the **[Choose Questions]** menu to proceed with scripted dialogue.
* For more detailed AI interaction troubleshooting, please refer to the [Troubleshooting Interview Audio and Microphone Issues](interview-issues.md) guide.

---

## 5. Cannot Drink Coffee / Scenario Won't Complete
**Issue:** You grabbed the coffee cup, but the scenario completion screen does not trigger.

**Solution:**
* Make sure you have completed **all previous objectives** in order (Wake up &rarr; Shower &rarr; Equip Prosthetic Arm &rarr; Talk to Mary/Pet Dog). The coffee objective is the final step.
* Ensure you are physically bringing the coffee cup close to your headset (mouth area) to trigger the drinking action.

---
