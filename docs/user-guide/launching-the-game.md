# Launching the Game

This guide shows you how to find and open DeVILSona on your Meta Quest headset.

---

## How to Launch DeVILSona

1. Put on the headset
2. Press the **Oculus button** on the right-hand controller to open the universal menu
3. Open the **App Library** (the grid icon at the bottom of the home screen)

    ![App Library](../educator-guide/img/app-library.png)

4. Click on the **Menu** button and navigate to the **"Unknown Sources"** tab — since DeVILSona was installed manually, it appears here instead of the main app list

    ![Menu View](../educator-guide/img/meta-menu-view.png)

5. Look for **"FSE100Capstone"** (or similar) in the list

    ![Unknown Sources View](../educator-guide/img/unknown-sources-view.png)

6. Tap it to launch the game — you should see the login screen

---

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't find the app?** Make sure you're looking under the **"Unknown Sources"** tab, not the main app list. DeVILSona won't appear in the regular library because it was sideloaded.
- **App crashes or shows a black screen?** The game files may not have been fully installed. Ask your instructor or lab staff to reinstall the app on the headset.
- **Login screen doesn't appear?** Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, let your instructor know.
