# Troubleshooting Navigation Issues

This page covers common navigation, positioning, and orientation issues that may occur while using the VR experience.

## Common Navigation Issues

Users may occasionally experience one or more of the following:

- feeling disoriented after teleporting in a scenario that uses teleport movement
- facing the wrong direction after a scene loads
- being positioned too far away from an object they need to interact with
- feeling slightly offset from where they are expected to stand or sit
- confusion about whether a scenario uses teleport movement at all

## Resetting Your View

If you appear in the wrong location or are facing the wrong direction, try resetting your view. The correct method depends on whether you are using the **desktop application through Meta Quest Link** or the **APK version** installed directly on the headset.

### Desktop Application Version (Meta Quest Link)

When playing the **desktop application through Meta Quest Link**, you can reset your orientation using either the Meta Quest Link menu or, if available in the current scenario, the in-game reset option.

**Option 1: Meta Quest Link Reset View**
1. Press the **Meta button** on the **right controller**.
2. Select **Reset View**.

This is typically the most direct way to re-center yourself while using the desktop application version.

**Option 2: In-Game Reset Orientation**
Some parts of the project also provide a **Reset Orientation** option through the in-game menu. This may be accessible through:

- the **watch widget** (**Y** button on **left controller** or **B** button on **right controller**)
- the **Menu** button with the **three horizontal lines** on the **left controller**

If this option is available and functional in the current scenario, it can also be used while playing the desktop application version.

### APK Version

When playing the **APK version** installed directly on the headset, pressing the **Meta button** does **not** provide a **Reset View** option for the scenario. Instead, it typically only provides the option to quit the application.

For the APK version, you must rely on the in-game reset option instead.

**In-Game Reset Orientation**
If available in the current scenario, use **Reset Orientation** through:

- the **watch widget** (**Y** button on **left controller** or **B** button on **right controller**)
- the **Menu** button with the **three horizontal lines** on the **left controller**

For the **APK version**, this is the primary method for resetting your orientation while remaining in the app.

## Teleportation and Disorientation

Some scenarios use **teleport movement**. If you are unfamiliar with VR teleport controls, it is normal to feel briefly disoriented after teleporting to a new position.

To reduce disorientation:

- pause briefly after teleporting before continuing
- re-check which direction you are facing before moving again
- use the appropriate reset method if you no longer feel aligned correctly
- avoid repeated rapid teleporting if you begin to feel uncomfortable

## Seated vs. Standing Play

For the best experience, choose either **seated** or **standing** play before starting a scenario and remain consistent throughout that session when possible.

Switching between sitting and standing during a scenario can sometimes affect your alignment, eye level, or distance from interactable objects. If this happens, use the appropriate reset option to re-center yourself.

## Scenario-Specific Movement Differences

Not all scenarios use navigation in the same way.

- Some scenarios require the player to **teleport manually** between areas.
- Some scenarios place the player automatically where they need to be.
- Some scenarios may support menu-based reset tools, while others may not.

If movement feels incorrect, first confirm whether the scenario is supposed to use teleportation or automatic positioning.

## If You Feel Out of Place in the Environment

If you are too close to an object, too far away, or facing the wrong direction:

1. Stop moving for a moment.
2. Check whether the scenario expects you to teleport or remain where you are.
3. Use the appropriate reset method for your platform:
   - **Desktop app through Meta Quest Link:** press the **Meta button** and select **Reset View**, or use **Reset Orientation** through the **watch widget** or **Menu** button if available
   - **APK version:** use **Reset Orientation** through the **watch widget** or **Menu** button
4. Reposition your real body slightly if needed.
5. If the issue continues, exit and restart the scenario.

## General VR Comfort Tips

Navigation problems can sometimes feel worse when combined with general VR discomfort. To reduce this:

- take a short break if you begin to feel dizzy, disoriented, or uncomfortable
- avoid rushing through repeated movement actions
- make sure your play area is clear and safe before continuing
- stop the experience if discomfort becomes significant

## When to Restart the Experience

Restarting the current scenario may help if:

- your position appears clearly incorrect after loading
- reset tools do not fix the issue
- your facing direction remains wrong
- you cannot reach an interaction point that should be within range

If restarting does not help, additional scenario-specific troubleshooting may be needed.
