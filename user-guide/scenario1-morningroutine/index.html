    Scenario 1: Morning Routine¶

    When the game starts, your objectives will be displayed on the screen. You will need to complete the following steps in order: Wake up, Take a shower, Equip your prosthetic arm, and Drink coffee.

    Note: Your right hand will remain invisible until you equip the prosthetic arm. However, for your convenience, a guide ray will still be visible when operating the UI.

    1. Waking Up¶

    • This is the most basic step designed to help you practice using the joystick.
    • Mission: Simply move to the designated location.
    • Movement: You will use Teleportation to navigate. Note that you can teleport through obstacles like glass doors.

    2. Taking a Shower¶

    • Move to the shower room. Place your left hand on the showerhead and press the interact button to turn on the water.
    • Water Temperature: The key to this task is adjusting the water temperature. Turn the dial to the left for hot water and to the right for cold water.
    • Completion: Successfully adjusting the temperature will fill up the gauge. Once the gauge is completely full, the shower task is complete. Press the button on the showerhead one more time to stop the water.

    3. Equipping the Prosthetic Arm¶

    * Approach the prosthetic arm hanging inside the shower room. * Grab it with your left hand. Once equipped, your right hand will visibly appear as the prosthetic arm.

    4. Interacting in the Living Room¶

    Mary (Wife NPC): Move to the living room where you will find Mary, Mike's wife. She is highly supportive of him. Approach her to initiate an interview interaction.

    Pet Dog: You can pet the dog on the head to trigger a simple, friendly interaction.

    5. Completing the Scenario¶

    • Finally, grab the coffee resting on the table.

    Doing this completes the Morning Routine scenario. A menu UI will then appear, prompting you to either proceed to the next scenario or return to the main menu.
